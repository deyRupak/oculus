What is OCULUS?


Helping the 'special ones' using the power of machine learning to detect ADHD,Autism and Dyslexia.The project aims early detection of 'Autism' and 'Dyslexia' through a web platform fueled by machine learning. It uses a standard computer webcam to screen a children while he/she sees photograph/s or reads a passage on the screen.

DETECTION

ADHD

A chronic condition including attention difficulty, hyperactivity and impulsiveness.

Take Test
AUTISM

A learning disorder characterised by difficulty reading.

Take Test

APPROACH


The project detects the child's pupil movement across the screen, hence giving a hint about his/her early symptoms of Autism & ADHD. It does this by plotting a graph based on the co-ordinates of the pupil recorded according to the viewport.
These points are then fed into a machine learning model trained to classify these patterns and thus showing the results as positive or negative against the opted test.
